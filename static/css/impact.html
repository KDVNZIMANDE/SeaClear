How to Reduce Your Impact on Polluted Waters

1. Proper Waste Disposal

Always dispose of waste properly by using designated waste bins. Avoid littering, and ensure that hazardous materials are disposed of at special facilities.

2. Reduce Use of Plastic

Minimize the use of single-use plastics like bags, bottles, and straws. Opt for reusable alternatives to reduce plastic pollution in water bodies.

3. Participate in Clean-Up Activities

Join local beach and river clean-up events to help remove trash from water bodies and prevent it from causing further pollution.

4. Educate Others

Spread awareness about the impact of pollution on water bodies and encourage others to adopt practices that reduce pollution.
